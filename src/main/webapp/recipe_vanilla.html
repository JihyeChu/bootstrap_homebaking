    Vanilla almond cream muffin

    muffin Ingredients

  • unsalted butter: 190g
  • Eggs (150g)
  • cake flour: 135g
  • ground almond: 90g
  • baking powder: 6g
  • sugar: 155g
  • warm milk: 120g

    • vanilla cream

  • fresh cream: 220g
  • vanilla paste: 3sp

    • 1. Combine unsalted butter, salt and sugar well.
    2. Mix it from time to time!
    3. Add egg mixture several times and mix.
    4. Sift together flour, baking powder, ground almond and mix.
    5. Lastly, add room temperature milk and mix it. That's it!
    6. After squeezing the dough into a mold, place it in the oven and bake it at 170 degrees for 30 minutes.
    7. I'm going to make vanilla cream. Melt the whipped cream and vanilla paste.
    10. Add the rest of the whipped cream and whip it well.
    11. Squeeze the whipped cream on top of the muffin.
         Enjoy your muffin!
